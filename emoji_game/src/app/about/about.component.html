How to Play

  • Type the word or phrase that the set of emojis correspond to
  • You win coins for each correct answer. Save up coins to buy hints!
  • Note: There are 3 types of hints. Keep pressing the hint button multiple times if you need more help!
    • 1st hint gives you a word count, and costs 1 coin
    • 2nd hint gives you the character count and costs 2 coins.
    • 3rd hint gives you the first letter of each word and costs 3 coins.
  • You have 3 lives. If you get 3 incorrect answers, you lose!
